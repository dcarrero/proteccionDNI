ID Protection

This page allows you to easily hide the private data of your ID. Sometimes you may be asked for a copy of your ID, but you should not send a photo as is because if it falls into the wrong hands it can cause serious problems.

Here you can block unnecessary data and overwrite text so they cannot use it for other purposes, this way the image you share will be unique to prevent reuse.

To start, click the choose photo button and continue with the following steps.
This entire process is done only in your browser, no data is sent anywhere.

Questions and answers

What does the page do?

It follows the steps recommended by police to protect your ID, without needing to remember all of them and know which data is important.

  1. Choose a photo of your ID. The image is converted to black and white and its size is limited to not give a high quality copy.
  2. Choose the ID format you have and if it's the front or back. Based on that, the data that no one needs to see is hidden.
  3. Adjust the position of the photo if necessary so it looks good in the preview.
  4. Add a text that will be overwritten as a watermark, indicating why you share the photo.
  5. Click the button to download the photo.
Why use it?

Sharing your ID carries the risk of it ending up in the wrong hands and suffering identity theft. In general, companies should not have access to most of your ID data.

If you're sure you need to send them your ID, what you should do is follow official guidelines, so that the copy is in black and white, hides the data that is not necessary to identify you and overwrites a text so that the use is unique. That's precisely what this page does automatically.

Can I trust you will not do anything with my ID?

The page does not store any data, no cookies are used, no third-party service is used and your ID is only processed on your phone/computer.

The source code can be viewed by pressing F12, it is not encrypted or minimized in any way. It's also available on GitHub.

Is it free?

Yes, using the page has no cost.
